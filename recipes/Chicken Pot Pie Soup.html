Chicken Pot Pie Soup

Description

All the ingredients for chicken pot pie -- the chunky chicken and vegetables and the savory broth -- are in this soup. A crisp crouton stands in for the pie crust.

Ingredients

  • Whole Rosemary Leaves
  • Whole Thyme Leaves
  • Garlic Powder
  • Butter
  • Mushrooms
  • Carrots
  • Flour
  • Chicken broth
  • Chicken thighs
  • Onions
  • Peas
  • Bread
  • Oil

Steps

  • Mix ingredients together in large pan
  • Gradually stir in broth
  • Cut bread into rounds with 3-inch cookie cutter
